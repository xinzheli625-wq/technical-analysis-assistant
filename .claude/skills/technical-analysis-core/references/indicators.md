# Technical Indicators Reference

## RSI (Relative Strength Index)

- **Period:** 14 (standard)
- **Overbought:** > 70 (may stay overbought in strong trends)
- **Oversold:** < 30
- **Divergence:** Price makes new high but RSI doesn't = warning
- **Best use:** Identify extremes + divergence, not timing

## MACD

- **Components:** MACD line (12EMA - 26EMA), Signal line (9EMA of MACD), Histogram
- **Bullish signal:** MACD crosses above signal line
- **Bearish signal:** MACD crosses below signal line
- **Zero line:** Above zero = bullish momentum, below = bearish
- **Divergence:** Price new high, MACD histogram lower = warning

## KDJ

- **K (fast), D (slow), J (most sensitive)**
- **Overbought:** K, D > 80
- **Oversold:** K, D < 20
- **Golden cross:** K crosses above D (buy)
- **Death cross:** K crosses below D (sell)
- **Best use:** Short-term momentum in ranging markets

## Bollinger Bands

- **Middle:** 20-period SMA
- **Upper/Lower:** 2 standard deviations
- **Squeeze:** Bands narrow → low volatility → expect expansion (big move)
- **Band walk:** Price rides upper/lower band = strong trend
- **Reversion:** Touch band ≠ reversal; wait for confirmation

## OBV (On-Balance Volume)

- **Calculation:** Volume added on up days, subtracted on down days
- **Leading indicator:** OBV divergence from price warns of trend change
- **Confirmation:** Price and OBV rising together = healthy trend
