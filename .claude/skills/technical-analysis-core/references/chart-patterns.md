# Chart Patterns Reference

## Western Classical Patterns

### Reversal Patterns

| Pattern | Requirements | Confirmation | Confidence |
|---------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| Head & Shoulders | 3 peaks, middle highest, shoulders similar height | Neckline break | High |
| Double Bottom | 2 similar lows, peak between them | Break above peak | Medium-High |
| Triple Bottom | 3 similar lows | Break above resistance | Medium |
| Rounding Bottom | Gradual curve, U-shape | Volume build on right side | Medium |

### Continuation Patterns

| Pattern | Requirements | Confirmation | Confidence |
|---------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| Cup with Handle | U-shaped cup (7-65 weeks), handle (-8% to -15%) | Break above handle | High |
| Flag | Strong pole, tight consolidation (5-15 days) | Break in pole direction | Medium-High |
| Pennant | Strong pole, converging triangle | Break in pole direction | Medium |
| Ascending Triangle | Flat top, rising bottom | Break above top | Medium |

## Japanese Candlestick Patterns

### Single Candle

| Pattern | Body | Wick | Signal | Confidence |
|---------|------|------|--------|-----------|
| Doji | Open ≈ Close | Long both sides | Indecision | Medium |
| Hammer | Small body at top | Long lower wick | Bullish reversal (at lows) | Medium-High |
| Shooting Star | Small body at bottom | Long upper wick | Bearish reversal (at highs) | Medium-High |
| Marubozu | Full body, no wicks | None | Strong directional | High |

### Two-Candle

| Pattern | First | Second | Signal | Confidence |
|---------|-------|--------|--------|-----------|
| Bullish Engulfing | Red (close < open) | Green body engulfs prev | Reversal | High |
| Bearish Engulfing | Green (close > open) | Red body engulfs prev | Reversal | High |
| Tweezer Bottom | Two matching lows | Matching lower wicks | Reversal | Medium |

### Three-Candle

| Pattern | Formation | Signal | Confidence |
|---------|----------|--------|-----------|
| Morning Star | Long red, small body, long green | Bullish reversal | High |
| Evening Star | Long green, small body, long red | Bearish reversal | High |
| Three White Soldiers | 3 consecutive green, each open > prev open | Strong bullish | High |
