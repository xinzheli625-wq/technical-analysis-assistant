# Fundamental Event Inference

## Principle

Technical patterns often reflect information asymmetry. Price moves before news is public because some participants know more.

## Inference Matrix

| Technical Signature | Likely Event | Confidence | Timeframe |
|--------------------|--------------|-----------|-----------|
| Sudden gap up + 3x volume + sustained | Earnings beat, M&A, major contract | High | Days |
| Gradual accumulation + volume build over weeks | Positioning ahead of catalyst | Medium | Weeks |
| Breakout after long base + sector rotation | Policy change, thematic shift | Medium | Weeks-Months |
| Steep decline on massive volume | Accounting issue, regulatory, earnings miss | High | Days |
| Repeated failed breakouts + distribution | Insider selling, lockup expiry | Medium | Weeks |
| VCP tightening + volume drying up | Pre-earnings quiet period | Medium | Days |
| Broadening formation + increasing volume | Uncertainty, pending decision | Medium | Variable |

## Caveats

- **This is inference, not fact.** Never trade on inferred events alone.
- **Verify with news.** If no news, the move may be technical or sector-driven.
- **Earnings season:** Inference accuracy drops; too much noise.
- **Macro shocks:** Can override all technical patterns (war, pandemic, etc.).
