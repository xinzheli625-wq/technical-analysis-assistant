# Capital Flow & Sentiment Interpretation

## Framework

Combine multiple technical signals to infer what "smart money" (institutions, insiders) is doing.

## Signal Interpretation Matrix

| Observation | Capital Behavior | Sentiment | Follow-through Likelihood |
|-------------|-----------------|-----------|--------------------------|
| Gap up + high volume | Institutional accumulation overnight | Strong bullish | High if held |
| Gradual uptrend, vol increasing | Sustained institutional buying | Building bullish | High |
| Sharp rise + vol spike + tight close | "Air refueling" - momentum consolidation | Bullish continuation | Medium-High |
| Declining volume during pullback | Float locked, no distribution | Healthy correction | High |
| Up on low vol, down on high vol | Distribution pattern | Bearish warning | Medium |
| Wide range, high vol, mid-close | Battle between buyers/sellers | Uncertainty | Low |
| Multiple gaps up, shrinking volume | Exhaustion / retail FOMO | Caution | Low |
| Decline on low volume | Lack of interest, not panic | Neutral/bearish | Low |
| Capitulation spike (massive vol, wide range) | Weak hands selling out | Potential bottom | Medium |

## Context Matters

- **Earnings week:** All signals amplified; be more cautious
- **Market correction:** Even strong setups can fail; reduce position sizes
- **New highs:** Volume should confirm; if not, distribution possible
- **Low float stocks:** Volume signals more significant (easier to manipulate)
